Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1t seasoned salt
  • ground chuck or sirloin
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 t pepper
  • 1+2½ can tomatoes (3 ½ C... large) [drain some liq.]
  • 1 8 oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 pkg. Spaghetti sauce mix
  • 1 ½ pound Lasagna noodles
  • ½ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced (or grated)
  • 1 ½ pound ricotta cheese
  • 1½ c grated parmesan cheese (optional)
  1. Brown meat, in seasoned salt.
  2. Add garlic, pepper, & simmer slowly ~10min.
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, Spaghetti sauce mix. Cover & simmer 30/min (or less).
    4. Meanwhile
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles & Preheat oven to 350°
  5. In a 12 x 8 x 2" dish:
    • Pour 1/3 sauce.
    • Cover with noodles.
    • Arrange mozzarella & spoons of ricotta.
    • Repeat the layers, ending with meat sauce.
    • OPTIONAL: top with parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake ~20 min (60 min. if cold), @350° Bake uncovered, with foil underneath pan
